Structure

📜 Week-1: Unlearn
From Day-1 we dive into the PYQs. We will attempt the questions and see what is required in the exam. During this week your previous notion about the exam will be reoriented.

🧠 Week-2: Absorb
In this week we focus deeper into the techniques. You will work on improving the skills required to crack UCEED/CEED.

👩🏻‍🏫 Week 3: Flipped Classroom
The best way to learn is to teach. This week you'll evaluate some work and assess the performance of other students.

📔 Week-4: Simplify & Reflect
We reflect on what we learned and try to find the answer to the fundamental question to excel in a competitive exam; ‘how to be different?’

The actual preparation required for the design entrance exam is over.

💡 Week-5: Preparation Starts
From the 5th week onward, revisit and practice the same topics again till the exam.

If you have dedicated the earlier 4 weeks with commitment, it is time to slow down.

What are the timings for the live sessions?
Timings are decided in consultation with the majority of the students. Generally, on weekdays sessions are in the evening after 7:00 PM.

Will I be able to watch the recorded sessions till the exam?
Yes, you have access to all the recordings till the exam or 1st March 2024 (whichever is earlier)

Is there a trial or demo session before I enroll?
Yes, you can enroll in a 1-on-1 trial session with a coach at Kaphal Studio to get answers for your doubts about the course and for general counseling on a career in design.
Please note that we charge a minimal amount for these trial sessions which is deducted if you enroll in the program.

Who will be my mentor?
All the mentors at Design Manual are professional mentors and are from premium design colleges.The mentors are trained in advance to help you achieve your goals.
